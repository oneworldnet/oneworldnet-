Our Impact Projects

We connect communities, support education, and empower humanitarian work with reliable satellite internet solutions.

Connecting Remote Schools

Free satellite internet for thousands of schools in remote and poor areas, empowering digital education for all children.

Hospitals & Clinics Support

Reliable connectivity for hospitals and clinics in underserved regions, enabling telemedicine and emergency care.

Universities & Research

Partnering with universities to provide high-speed internet for research, innovation, and global collaboration.

Humanitarian & Refugee Camps

Connecting humanitarian camps and NGOs to enable effective relief, education, and health services.

Solar-Powered Internet

Deploying solar-powered receivers for sustainable, green connectivity in off-grid and rural communities.

Blockchain Security

World’s highest privacy: all data encrypted with blockchain, making it unbreakable and inaccessible to any third party.

Nano Receiver Project

Miniaturizing our satellite receiver to the size of a SIM card for the next generation of ultra-portable connectivity.

Global Partnerships

Partnering with leading tech companies to deliver free internet for all humanity in the future.

10Gbps for Asia & China

Delivering record-breaking speeds up to 10Gbps in Asia and China, setting new standards for global connectivity.
